Highlights Of the Places, We Will Take You

Temple Of tooth Relic

Located in the heart of Kandy, Sri Lanka, the Temple of the Sacred Tooth Relic is one of the most significant religious and cultural landmarks in the country.

This sacred Buddhist temple houses a revered relic of Lord Buddha’s tooth, making it a vital pilgrimage site for Buddhists worldwide.

Anuradhapura
The Sacred City of Sri Lanka

Anuradhapura, located in the North Central Province of Sri Lanka, is one of the oldest and most sacred cities in the country

Once the capital of ancient Sri Lanka, it is now a UNESCO World Heritage Site and a major pilgrimage destination, renowned for its spiritual, historical, and archaeological significance.

London Clock Tower

The London Clock Tower, more commonly known as the Elizabeth Tower, is an iconic symbol of the United Kingdom.

Visitors can marvel at its historical significance and its striking presence against the London skyline.

Located at the northern end of the Palace of Westminster, this stunning structure is famous worldwide for housing the famous Big Ben bell.
